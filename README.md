# Cryptvest - High-Gain Investment Platform

Cryptvest is a web-based investment platform designed to provide users with high-profit, short-term investment opportunities while maintaining trust and transparency. Users can sign up, manage their accounts, deposit and withdraw funds, and track their investments with a dynamic, interactive dashboard.

## Features

- **Authentication:** Secure Sign In / Sign Up system with username/email login.
- **User Profiles:** Personal details, country selection, and contact support link.
- **Wallet Management:** Deposit and withdrawal in BTC and SOL with wallet copy functionality.
- **Investment Management:** Invest funds with a potential high-gain calculator and interactive Chart.js visualization.
- **Transaction History & Notifications:** Track all deposits, withdrawals, and investments with real-time notifications.
- **Local Storage:** Save user accounts, balances, history, and notifications in the browser.
- **Responsive Design:** Works smoothly across desktop and mobile devices.
